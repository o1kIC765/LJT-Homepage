C-Eval is a comprehensive multi-level multi-discipline Chinese evaluation suite for foundation models. It consists of 13,948 multi-choice questions across 52 disciplines and 4 difficulty levels, providing a thorough evaluation of Chinese language understanding and reasoning capabilities.

Co-authors: Yuzhen Huang, Yuzhuo Bai, Zhihao Zhu, Junlei Zhang, Jinghan Zhang, Tangjun Su, Chuancheng Lv, Yikai Zhang, Jiayi Lei, Yao Fu, Maosong Sun, Junxian He.
