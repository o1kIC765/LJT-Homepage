This paper presents SynLogic, a scalable framework for synthesizing verifiable reasoning data. We generate a large-scale dataset of over 1M reasoning examples that can be automatically verified, enabling efficient learning of logical reasoning capabilities. The approach shows significant improvements on various reasoning benchmarks.

Co-authors: Yuanxiang Fan, Zhuo Jiang, Han Ding, Yongyi Hu, Chi Zhang, Yiqi Shi, Shitong Weng, Aili Chen, Shiqi Chen, Yunan Huang, Mozhi Zhang, Pengyu Zhao, Junjie Yan, Junxian He.
