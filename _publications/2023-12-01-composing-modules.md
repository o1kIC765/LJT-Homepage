This work introduces a method to compose parameter-efficient modules using arithmetic operations. We show that modules learned for different tasks can be combined through simple arithmetic operations (addition, subtraction) to create new modules that achieve strong performance on unseen tasks.

Co-authors: Jinghan Zhang, Shiqi Chen, Junxian He.
